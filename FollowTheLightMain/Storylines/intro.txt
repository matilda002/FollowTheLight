Awakening in pitch-black oblivion, memories lost to the void, a bone-chilling cold grips the air. 
Screams reverberate, swallowed by the oppressive dark. A faint device flickers, its voice pleading, “Who’s there? Where am I?” 
An unsettling truth lingers—strangers, bound by this abyss, must collaborate to escape.
No past, no exit, just an uneasy pact in this nightmarish hell. Can you unravel the shadows together, or be devoured by the creatures of your own fear? 
The game begins, and only unity can survive the lurking horrors...
